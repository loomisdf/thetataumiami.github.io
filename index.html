Theta Tau

The Nation's Oldest and Largest Professional Engineering Fraternity

Service

Since our certification on January 26, 2013, the members of the Miami University colony of Theta Tau have been working diligently to establish our selves as an influential organization - both within Miami University and the community of Oxford, OH. As engineers, our members understand that it is our civic duty to serve and inspire those around us.

Professional Development

Our vision as a fraternity is to create an environment in which our members can exercise their natural skills as students and leaders, while also developing their skills as future employees. With a unique professional emphasis on both the present and the future, we allow for our members to focus on today, but prepare for tomorrow.

Brotherhood

Our members pride themselves upon sharing a brotherhood unlike any other - one built around academic achievement, professional preparation, and a mutual desire for success. With all of this brotherhood comes the development of lasting friendships that not only strengthen the achievements of our fraternity, but foster a mindset of social activism, which allows for our brothers to branch out and succeed not only in the classroom and the workplace, but everywhere they go.
